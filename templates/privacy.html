Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how pricesareup.com collects and uses information when you visit the website.

Information collected automatically

When you visit the website, hosting providers automatically collect certain information in their server logs. This may include:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • ISP (Internet Service Provider)
  • Date and time of your visit
  • The pages you visit

This data is used for security, performance monitoring, and debugging purposes.

Cookies and Local Storage

Cookes and local storage are used to improve your experience. They aren't used for tracking or advertising.

Type Name Purpose
Cookie timezone Stores your local timezone so dates are displayed correctly for your region. (365 day expiry)
Local Storage theme Remembers your preference for dark or light mode.
Local Storage crazyMode Remembers if you have "Crazy Mode" enabled.

Third-Party Services

Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) are used to load standard libraries faster. These requests may expose your IP address to these third parties:

  • jsDelivr & Cloudflare CDN: Used to load Bootstrap, FontAwesome, and Chart.js.
